# Linh Dang

Linh discovered yoga during college as a way to manage stress and has been practicing for over 15 years. With certifications in Hatha, Yin, and Prenatal yoga, she specializes in creating accessible classes for beginners.

## Teaching Philosophy

"I believe yoga should be accessible to everyone. My classes focus on proper alignment, mindful movement, and creating a supportive environment where students feel comfortable exploring their practice at their own pace."

## Certifications

- 200-Hour Yoga Teacher Training, Yoga Alliance
- 85-Hour Prenatal Yoga Certification
- 50-Hour Yin Yoga Training

## Class Schedule

- Mondays & Wednesdays, 6-7pm: Beginners Yoga
- Fridays, 9-10am: Gentle Morning Flow
- Saturdays, 11am-12pm: Prenatal Yoga

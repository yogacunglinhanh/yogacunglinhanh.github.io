# Beginners Yoga

This six-week course introduces the fundamentals of yoga in a supportive, accessible environment. Perfect for those new to yoga or looking to refine their understanding of the basics.

## What You'll Learn

- Foundational yoga poses with proper alignment
- Basic breathing techniques
- Introduction to meditation
- How to use props to support your practice
- Yoga philosophy and mindfulness principles

## Course Structure

Each 60-minute class builds progressively, allowing you to develop strength, flexibility, and confidence at a comfortable pace.

**Week 1:** Introduction to yoga basics and conscious breathing
**Week 2:** Standing poses and balance
**Week 3:** Hip openers and forward bends
**Week 4:** Backbends and twists
**Week 5:** Inversions and arm balances (modified for beginners)
**Week 6:** Bringing it all together - creating a home practice

## What to Bring

- Comfortable clothing
- Water bottle
- Yoga mat (mats available to borrow if needed)
- Open mind and kind attitude toward yourself

This course is led by Sarah Johnson, a certified yoga instructor with 10+ years of experience specializing in teaching beginners.

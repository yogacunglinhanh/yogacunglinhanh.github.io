# Dynamic Vinyasa Flow

This intermediate course focuses on linking breath with movement in flowing sequences that build strength, flexibility, and mindfulness.

## Course Highlights

- Creative flowing sequences
- Advanced variations of fundamental poses
- Pranayama (breathwork) practices
- Core strengthening and arm balances
- Peak poses with preparation and cool-down

## Prerequisites

Students should have at least 6 months of regular yoga practice and familiarity with basic poses.

## Your Instructor

Michael Chen brings 8 years of teaching experience with specialized training in vinyasa and power yoga traditions.
